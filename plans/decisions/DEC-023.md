# DEC-023: Lower S/R Strength Score Filter from 60 to 40

**Date:** May 13, 2026
**Status:** ACTIVE
**Severity:** HIGH — causes quality score to miss +25 nearSR bonus frequently

## Problem

`technicalAnalysis.service.ts` line 277 filters S/R levels at `strengthScore >= 60`. The strength formula requires 5+ touches, high volume, and strong rejection wicks for full points. Many valid S/R levels score 40-59 and are discarded. Without levels, `nearSR = false` and the +25 quality bonus is lost, often dropping the total score below the 60 threshold.

## Decision

Lower the filter threshold from 60 to 40. Levels scoring 40-59 are still validated S/R zones (2+ touches, some volume) but not as strong as 60+.

Additionally, introduce a tiered usage:
- strengthScore >= 60: Used for TP/SL calculation (high confidence)
- strengthScore 40-59: Used for quality scoring only (nearSR bonus), NOT for TP/SL

## Impact

Files modified: `technicalAnalysis.service.ts` line 277, plus split S/R query into two lists
Files modified: `tpslCalculatorV2.service.ts` — uses only `strongLevels` (>= 60)
Zero breaking changes — only threshold adjustment and list splitting.

## Guardrails

- TP/SL calculator still only uses levels >= 60 for actual price targets
- Quality scoring gains access to more levels for proximity detection
- No new database columns or tables
- Strength score formula itself is unchanged
